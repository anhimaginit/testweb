How to add or update contact

Add Contact

Click on contact and fill out the form

If the company is not exist in system, you can click on button to add company

You need to fill email. If the email is exist, you cannot add new contact but you can click on the link under email field to view the contact.

You can select types as Sales, Policy Holder, Employee, Vendor, Affiliate.

With Sales: You select states to activate locations.

With Vendor or Affiliate: You choose subtypes.

Step 2: After fill all the infomation, click on to finish

Update Contact

When you update contact informations, You can view some table: Note, Orders, Claims, Warranties, Contact Document.

With Note and Contact Document: You can add or edit new note when click on cell at column `Note` and change flag.

With Orders, Claims and Warranties: You can click to go to page of them. If you want to create new, you can click on button at the right.

When you change street address 1, the location in map will be reset.

You can view emails which system send for the contact, view detail and resend mails.

If you want to change the username, click on and enter new email. After change, click on to apply. You need to enter password to confirm change. To revert username, click on
