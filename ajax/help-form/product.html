how to create or update product

Click on add product on the left sidebar and fill on the form

Product name should be clean and light, descripted main features

The SKU must be unique in system. When you enter SKU, system will check valid SKU.

You can choose image for product and preview this image on preview frame below the Product Photo field.

For description, you can provide more informations, specifications of product. Limit 2000 characters

Product has one of types: Physical and Digital.

Product has one of classes: A La Carte, Discount, Marketing, Warranty. Among them, A La Carte and Warranty class have warranty.

You need to provide price, weight, height, width, length (Number) and other fields

Taxable, Public Visible, Internal Visible, Inactive are options

  • Taxable: The product price include taxable
  • Public Visible: The product is public for everyone
  • Internal Visible: The product is internal
  • Inactive: Disable the product

After fill, click on to save
