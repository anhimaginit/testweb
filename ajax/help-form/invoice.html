how to add or update invoice

Field Descriptions
Invoice Invoice Number Number Enter invoice number. The invoice number must be unique in system. When you enter invoice, system will check valid invoice number.
Customer Select one Search customer who pay this invoice. When choose customer, the order will be get this customer order.
Salesperson Select one Select salesperson
Order Select one Select order need to pay for the invoice
Order infomation Table Table show products in order.
Beside, show total payment amount, balance, some fee transaction (if valid),...
Ledger Table Table show ledger paid by that invoice
Schedule Payments Table Table will show next schedule payment.

Historical Payments Table If the order have other invoice and paid at least 1 time, History Payment table will show them.

Step by Step

Step 1:

Click on button in invoice form

Step 2:

Fill on payment form and submit
Field Description
Method Select One Select payment method
Includes: Cash, Check, Acct and CC
Payment Amount Number Enter the payment amount which you want to pay that time.
If the payment amount is larger than balance, You need to choose one of following types:
  • Add Overage to Acct to accept payment with amount larger than balance, then the larger part would be called Overage
  • Adjust Payment to fill the balance to pay that time.
  • Cancel to cancel operation
