how to create or edit group

Condition: Has permission
Navigation administrator group

In group, You can view your role and unit

The required group informations are:

  • Unit:

    Setup unit group. Select from box, include: Sales, Affiliate, Customer, Vendor, Employee, Policy Holder

  • Group name: Setup group name
  • Role:

    Setup role for group. Include: User, Leader, Manager, Administrator (Admin)

  • Users: Members in group. A user may be join in multiple groups

The collection make from | Unit | Group name | Role | must be unique in system

Group may be have parent group and supervisor.

After setup, click on to create group or to update group
