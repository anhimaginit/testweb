How to add or update claim

Add Claim

You need to select Customer, Warranty to start claim. Vendor is options

Click on to save

After start, Claim is ready to use.

Update Claim

After started claim, Some tasks will be automatic creation. You need to provide informations to complete claim

Field Descriptions
Assigned Select one Select Assigned person, who manage and get responsibility for this claim
Start date Date
End date Date
Status Select 1 Check claim status. After edit, if it not change, default it is changed from Open to In progress
Claim Assign Task Tasks for claim.
To push new task, click on and fill infomations for new task
Quote Quote Amount
Quote Number
Quote date
Approved Quote		 Employee need to enter values for quote. Customer will Approve quote or Decline quote
Invoice Invoice Amount
Invoice Number
Invoice date
Approved Invoice		 Employee need to enter values for quote. Customer will Approve invoice or Decline invoice
Claim Limits If you have control transaction permission for that claim, You can enter payout amount for the claim limit.
If payout amount is larger than available, You need to override or contact to supervisor to accept.
Acctant will Approved pay or Decline pay for current payment
Send mail

To send mail for assigned person, click on on Note table

To send mail for assigned task person, click on on Tasks table
